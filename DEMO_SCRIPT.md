# 🎙️ StoreOS: High-Impact Demo Script

> **Goal**: Convince the judges that StoreOS isn't just a dashboard—it's an autonomous agent that saves money while you sleep.

## 🕒 Phase 1: The "Mission Control" Reveal (0:00 - 0:45)
*   **Action**: Open `localhost:5173/` (the dashboard).
*   **Script**: 
    > "Meet StoreOS. In the next 3 minutes, we're going to show you how skip-level operational intelligence can eliminate the 'Silent Loss' that kills dark stores. Right now, you're looking at our **Global Mission Control**. That ticker at the top? That's not just a number—it's the live financial drain across our Koramangala, Indiranagar, and HSR clusters. We are currently losing ₹173 every hour to inefficiencies that manual managers simply can't see."

## 🎤 Phase 2: Agentic Entry (0:45 - 1:30)
*   **Action**: Click the Mic icon.
*   **Script**:
    > "But we don't fix this with spreadsheets. We fix it with Agentic AI. I'll ask my system to prioritize our intervention."
*   **Command**: Speak clearly: `"Show me critical alerts"`
*   **Response**: The system navigates to the Alerts Control Room.
*   **Script**:
    > "StoreOS just parsed the entire network telemetry. It's identified **Decision Conflicts** where our automated reorder systems are clashing with actual inventory expiry. Look at this alert: The system wants to reorder milk, but StoreOS knows it's about to expire. It's blocked the reorder and recommended a 20% flash discount instead."

## 🔍 Phase 3: Deep-Dive Telemetry (1:30 - 2:15)
*   **Action**: Click the Mic icon.
*   **Command**: Speak clearly: `"Monitor Koramangala"`
*   **Script**:
    > "Let's deep-dive into a single node. StoreOS is pulling live Thermal Matrix data from our cold storage. We have an ML-powered risk score of 10/10 here. The compressor is thermal-throttling, and we have ₹47/hr of silent loss. StoreOS is giving us **Cognitive Override Options**—autonomous actions we can take right now to recover that margin."

## ⚡ Phase 4: The Hero Resolution (2:15 - 3:00)
*   **Action**: Click the Mic icon.
*   **Command**: Speak clearly: `"Resolve all"`
*   **Script**:
    > "Instead of manually clicking buttons, I'm going to let the Agent handle the entire cluster. 'Resolve all' initiates global mitigation protocols. It's applying discounts, stopping bad reorders, and alerting maintenance—all autonomously. We've just saved this network ₹1,200 in margin leakage, hands-free. This is the future of dark-store operations. This is StoreOS."
